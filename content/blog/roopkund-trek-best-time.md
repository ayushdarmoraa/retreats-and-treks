# Best Time to Do the Roopkund Trek

Planning your Roopkund Trek? Choosing the right month is crucial for the best experience. Here’s a month-by-month breakdown to help you decide:

## Month-wise Breakdown

### May
- **Weather:** Snow patches, cool temperatures, clear skies.
- **Trail:** Some snow at higher altitudes, rhododendron blooms.
- **Why Go:** Classic Himalayan spring, great for snow lovers.
- [See Roopkund in May](/treks/roopkund/may)

### June
- **Weather:** Warming up, snow melting, lush meadows.
- **Trail:** Green landscapes, some muddy patches.
- **Why Go:** Meadows at their best, fewer crowds than May.

### July–August
- **Weather:** Monsoon, heavy rain, landslide risk.
- **Trail:** Slippery, leeches, not recommended.
- **Why Avoid:** Safety concerns, poor visibility.

### September
- **Weather:** Post-monsoon clarity, fresh air, green valleys.
- **Trail:** Clear, vibrant, minimal snow.
- **Why Go:** Best for photographers, stable weather.

### October
- **Weather:** Crisp, cold, clear blue skies.
- **Trail:** Golden meadows, snow returns at summit.
- **Why Go:** Autumn colors, best views.
- [See Roopkund in October](/treks/roopkund/october)

### November–April
- **Weather:** Harsh winter, heavy snow, closed trails.
- **Trail:** Inaccessible, dangerous.
- **Why Avoid:** Trek is closed for safety.

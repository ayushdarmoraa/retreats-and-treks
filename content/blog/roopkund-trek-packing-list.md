# Roopkund Trek Packing List

Packing right is key to a safe and enjoyable Roopkund Trek. Here’s a categorized list of what you need:

## Clothing
- 2–3 quick-dry trekking shirts
- 2 trekking pants
- 1 warm fleece or down jacket
- 1 rain jacket/poncho
- Thermal base layers (top & bottom)
- Woolen cap, gloves, neck gaiter
- 3–4 pairs of socks (woolen + synthetic)

## Gear
- 40–50L backpack
- Trekking shoes (ankle support)
- Headlamp with extra batteries
- Trekking poles
- Sunglasses (UV protection)
- Water bottles (2L total)
- Sleeping bag (if not provided)

## Essentials
- Sunscreen (SPF 30+), lip balm
- Personal medicines, first aid kit
- Toiletries (biodegradable soap, toothbrush, etc.)
- Power bank
- ID proof (original + copy)
- Snacks (energy bars, dry fruits)

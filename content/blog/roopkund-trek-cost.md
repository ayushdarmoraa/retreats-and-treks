# Roopkund Trek Cost

**How much does the Roopkund trek cost in 2026?**

If you’re searching for the real Roopkund trek cost, you’re in the right place. Here’s the full breakdown—no fluff, just facts.

## Roopkund Trek Cost (2026)

Roopkund trek cost ranges from **₹12,000 to ₹18,000** per person. The price depends on the operator, inclusions, group size, and season.

### What’s Included
- All meals during trek
- Camping and tent accommodation
- Trek leader, guide, and support staff
- Permits and safety equipment

### What’s Not Included
- Travel to/from Lohajung
- Personal gear
- Insurance

## Cost Factors
- **Season:** May–June and September–October are peak (higher price)
- **Operator:** Premium operators charge more for safety, food, and staff
- **Group Size:** Smaller groups may cost more per person
- **Inclusions:** Check if permits, meals, and gear are included

## Roopkund Trek Cost Comparison
- Roopkund: ₹12,000–₹18,000
- Brahmatal: ₹8,500–₹12,500

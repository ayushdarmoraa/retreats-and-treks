# Burnout Recovery Retreat Experience: What People Actually Feel

## Signs of Burnout

Burnout is more than just feeling tired. It’s chronic exhaustion, loss of motivation, sleep problems, and a sense of being overwhelmed by even small tasks. Many professionals, founders, and remote workers experience burnout without realizing how deeply it affects their lives.

### Common Signs
- Trouble sleeping or staying asleep
- Emotional numbness or irritability
- Difficulty focusing
- Feeling disconnected from work or relationships
- Physical symptoms: headaches, muscle tension, digestive issues

## What Retreat Experience Is Like

Burnout recovery retreats in the Himalayas are designed to create space for genuine rest and recalibration. Unlike a vacation, these retreats offer structured programs that address both mind and body:

- **Guided journaling and reflection**
- **Breathwork and nervous system regulation**
- **Nature immersion**: forest walks, mountain air
- **Group support and sharing circles**
- **Digital detox**
